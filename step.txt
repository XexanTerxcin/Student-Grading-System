The prompt won't close nor move to the next prompt untill the user gives any input.
The input value in Physics, Chemistry, Math and English cannot be greater than 100 an less than 0.

If any of the input value is more than 100 or less than 0 the Process() function won't work

And in average it will be "y[1,2,3,4,5]", where y=x[1,2,3,4,5]/4.
